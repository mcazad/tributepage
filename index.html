Dr. Richard Feynman

I would rather have questions that can't be answered than answers that can't be questioned --- Dr. Richard Feynman

Here is a brief timline of Dr. Feynman's major life events.

  • 1918 - Born in Queens, NY
  • 1939 - Received BS in Physics from MIT
  • 1942 - Awarded his PhD in Physics from Princeton
  • 1942 - Married Arline Greebaum
  • 1943 - Worked with Robert Oppenheimer on The Manhattan Project at Los Alamos
  • 1945 - Wife Arline died
  • 1954 - Received Albert Einstein Award from the Atomic Energy Commission
  • 1962 - Received AEC's Ernest Orlando Lawrence Award
  • 1965 - Received Nobel Prize for Physics
  • 1965 - Elected as a Foreign Member of the Royal Society
  • 1972 - Received the Oerstead Medal from Denmark
  • 1986 - Member of Presidential Commission on the Space Shuttle Challenger Disaster
  • 1988 - Died in Los Angeles, CA
